How to configure feed product in shopify

Step 1

The app has 4 tabs for different ad sources: Facebook Feed (Instagram), Google Feed, Yottos Feed and Pinterest feed.

Install app for free

Step 2

To customize settings of each of these sections you can use such fields as "Your Collection", and "Your UTM tag"

Install app for free

Step 3

Besides that, there are also two more fields present: "... product feed set up" and "your app option".

Install app for free

Step 4

Your collection" field is used for selecting products, which is in your stock, and putting them in the generated new feed.

Install app for free

Step 5

"Your UTM tag" field is needed for utm-tagging, which will be also generated for all products in the feed.

Install app for free

Step 6

"Your app option" field shows you all available options in your application (contents may vary due to the subscription type).

Install app for free

Step 7

"... product feed set up" shows you the final feed, with all the settings made in Feed Product and all subscription functions you have.
