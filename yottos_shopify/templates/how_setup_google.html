HOW TO CONNECT FEED PRODUCT TO GOOGLE

STEP 1

To upload Feed Product into the merchants.google.com

Go to merchants.google.com

And move to "Products" section in the opened window.

Install app for free

STEP 2

In this section select "Multiple products" option and click on "Create Product feed".

Install app for free

STEP 3

In the "Basic information" section fill in your Country and Language info.

Install app for free

STEP 4

When all fields are filled, click "Continue".

Install app for free

STEP 5

In the "Name and input method" section you will need to fill in the name of the new field.

Install app for free

STEP 6

You will also need to select "Scheduled fetch" method.

Install app for free

STEP 7

When all these fields are filled, click "Continue".

Install app for free

STEP 8

In the "Setup" section you will need to fill in:

1. Imported file's name

2. Update frequency (Daily, Weekly, Monthly).

3. Custom update frequency.

4. Time zone.

5. Put here Data Feed's URL, that was made for you by Feed Product app.

Install app for free

STEP 9

Fields filled!

Install app for free

STEP 10

Click on "Create feed"

Install app for free

STEP 11

Feed is created, and you can check it in the "Product" section of "Feeds" tab.

Install app for free
